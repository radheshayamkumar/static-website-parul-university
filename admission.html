Admission Process

At Parul University, we provide a streamlined admission process to ensure the best candidates are selected for each program. We follow both online and offline admission procedures. Here's how to apply:

  1. Visit our admission portal.
  2. Fill out the online application form with your personal and academic details.
  3. Upload required documents like mark sheets, photographs, etc.
  4. Attend an entrance test or interview (if applicable) for specific courses.
  5. Complete the payment of admission fees once selected.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact our Admissions Office at admissions@paruluniversity.ac.in.
