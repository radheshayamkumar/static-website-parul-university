About Parul University

Founded in 2000, Parul University has been a leader in providing quality education in India. We offer a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs with an emphasis on practical learning and research.

Located in Vadodara, Gujarat, our university is known for its diverse and inclusive campus, outstanding faculty, and modern infrastructure.

We focus on holistic development, preparing students not just academically, but also for professional and personal growth.
